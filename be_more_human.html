Reebok: Brand Campaign

Be More Human

“Be More Human” was the world’s reintroduction to Reebok. A new rallying cry for the tough fitness athlete, we created a campaign that looked like the antithesis of anything from either Nike or Adidas. Colors were desaturated, designs were distressed, and the photography was gritty. The type looked like it was chiseled in granite. Everything feels tactile, imperfect, and above all else, human.

Human Experience

Online, we created The Be More Human: Experience, a site comprised of 3 distinct sections, exploring the correlation between fitness and humanity. With “Human Score,” we partnered with researchers to create an online quiz that scored a person’s humanness. In “Gray Matters,” we worked with neuroscientists to create an interactive visualizer that measured the effect of exercise on the brain. And finally with “Break Your Selfie,” we flipped the idea of the perfectly beautiful, curated selfie by encouraging the public to post their sweaty, post-workout selves.
