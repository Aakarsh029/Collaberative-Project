NBCUniversal: Integrated Campaign

Creative We Stand

In partnership with NBCUniversal, we created a campaign to celebrate the work of activists using their creativity in the ongoing fight against hate. Featuring a soundtrack by Princess Nokia and legendary hip-hop producer Timbaland, our film highlights the work of real-life change makers who prove activism can go far beyond protests and picket signs. The track/campaign was launched in a big way on The Today Show and has since been featured on E! News, Deadline, XXL, Highsnobiety, Fader, and The Hollywood Reporter.
