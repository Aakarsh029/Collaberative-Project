Perrier-Jouët: Brand Activation

L'Eden Art Basel

Perrier-Jouët perpetuates its long-standing ties with the world of art and creation by partnerning with selected design fairs around the globe. In 2015, the champagne house collaborated with Japanese Artist Ritsue Mishima to launch ‘L’Eden by Perrier-Jouët’ in Art Basel Miami.
Hosting an array of immersive experiences through artistic journeys, L’Eden is a wild enchanting escape resident to Miami’s most iconic Faena Hotel.
